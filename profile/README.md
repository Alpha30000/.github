# Alpha30000

> In search of alpha across fragmented markets.

---

## About

AlphaJourney is a quantitative research group focused on discovering and building **systematic trading strategies** in financial markets.

We explore inefficiencies across fragmented venues — particularly in cryptocurrency markets — and develop **data-driven, repeatable decision systems** rather than relying on discretionary trading.

---

## What We Do

- Quantitative research & factor modeling  
- Cross-sectional long-short strategies  
- Multi-exchange data integration  
- Systematic trading infrastructure (OMS, data pipelines)  
- Backtesting and live execution systems  

---

## Focus Areas

- Crypto markets (primary)
- Cross-exchange alpha
- High-frequency data (minute-level and beyond)
- Execution systems and market microstructure

---

## Philosophy

- **Alpha is not prediction — it is structure**
- **Execution matters as much as signal**
- **Reproducibility over intuition**
- **Systems over one-off ideas**
